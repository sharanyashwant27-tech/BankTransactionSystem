How it works

  • Salary credits appear instantly on the recipient account as a credit transaction.
  • Performance incentives use rating tiers: Outstanding ($1,500), Excellent ($1,000), Good ($500), Satisfactory ($250).
  • You can credit salary and incentives to employees or to your own administrator account.
  • Recipients can view credits in Transaction History but cannot delete administrative credits.
